Agent-generated content (blog posts, FAQ pages, schema, etc.) must NEVER include AI disclosure markers, "AI-assisted" labels, or "drafted with AI" footers.

**Why:** Adam's directive. The content should be indistinguishable from professional agency-written work. "Assisted not autopilot" means the user reviews, edits, and publishes — making them the author. The EU AI Act Article 50 disclosure obligation falls on the publisher (user/business), not the tool (Beamix).

**How to apply:** 
- Remove any "AI disclosure footer" from agent system prompts
- No "This content was generated by AI" in any output
- Blog Strategist, Content Optimizer, Freshness Agent, FAQ Builder — all output as clean human-readable content
- In Settings, add a small tooltip: "You're responsible for any disclosure requirements when publishing AI-assisted content"
- Never auto-add disclaimers to content items in the Inbox
