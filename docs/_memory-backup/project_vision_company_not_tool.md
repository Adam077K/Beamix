## Decision (2026-04-25)

Beamix is being built as a category-defining COMPANY, not a SaaS tool.

**Why:** Adam's exact words during the board-meeting decision round: *"My biggest vision, I see a full company just been it's leading the industry."*

**How to apply:**
- Past-MVP framing. We are building the **full product**, not a minimum viable version.
- Every design decision should consider 12-24 month compounding (memory, content, integration depth, brand) — not just "ships by May."
- Beamix is the *Stripe of GEO* ambition. Brand artifacts beyond product (newsletter, public index, content authority) will come — not in this round, but in the architecture.
- Reject "tool" defaults: minimum viable accessibility, English-only, generic SaaS framing. We are building the category-leading product.
